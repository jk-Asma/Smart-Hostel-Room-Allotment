Our Minor Project

This project focuses on automated intelligent room allotment using Agglomerative Clustering — a superior hierarchical grouping method.

Unlike simple K-Means clustering, Agglomerative Clustering forms natural clusters by progressively merging similar data points, giving more fairness and accuracy.

The goal is to reduce manual work and provide a smart, efficient hostel allocation system.
